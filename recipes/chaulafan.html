Chaulafan

What is it?

Chaulafan is an Ecuadorean dish that is made of rice, vegetables, and proteins like pork, beef and chicken, with Oriental seasoning.

The result is a flavorful, colorful, and hearty dish that reflects the cultural fusion between Ecuadorian and Chinese gastronomy.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Pork
  • Beef
  • Onions
  • Eggs
  • Chicken
  • Sesame oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Bell peppers
  • Garlic

How to make it:

  1. Cook the rice with anticipation and let it cool
  2. Chop the pork, beef and chicken into small pieces and season them
  3. Use a wok to heat the oil and start stir-frying the meats until fully cooked, remove and set aside
  4. In the same wok, do the same step but now for the vegetables
  5. Add the beaten eggs and stir until cooked
  6. Now mix the rice with the prepared ingredients
  7. Add the soy sauce and the sesame oil mixing consistently
  8. Serve the plate and decorate with a green chopped onion
  9. Enjoy!
