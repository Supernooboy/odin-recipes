Salchipapa

What is it?

Salchipapa is a popular Latin American street food made with fried sausages and French fries.

It is a simple, tasty, and filling dish, commonly served with different sauces and enjoyed as a quick meal or snack.

Ingredients:

  • Sausages
  • Potatoes
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo
  • Mustard

How to make it:

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes into strips
  2. Heat oil and fry the potatoes until golden and crispy, then set aside
  3. Slice the sausages into pieces
  4. Fry the sausages until lightly browned
  5. Mix the fries and sausages together on a plate
  6. Add salt and serve with ketchup, mayonnaise, and mustard
  7. Enjoy!
