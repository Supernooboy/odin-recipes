Cevichocho

What is it?

Cevichocho is a traditional Ecuadorian dish made with lupini beans marinated in lime juice and mixed with fresh vegetables and seasonings.

It is a refreshing, light, and nutritious dish, commonly eaten as street food and especially popular in the Andean region.

Ingredients:

  • Lupini Beans
  • Lime juice
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Cilantro
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil
  • Avocado
  • Chili (Optional)

How to make it?

  1. Wash and finely slice the onions, then rinse them with salt and water
  2. Chop the tomatoes and cilantro
  3. In a bowl, mix the lupini beans with lime juice and a little oil
  4. Add the tomatoes, onions and cilantro and mix it well
  5. Adjust salt and add chili if you prefer it
  6. Serve the dish and add avocado slices to decorate
  7. Enjoy!
